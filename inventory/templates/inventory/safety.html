Do's

Showcase fireworks in accordance with the cautions and directions specified on the packaging.
Purchase fireworks exclusively from the manufacturer or an authorized dealer.
It is crucial to consistently adhere to the safety guidelines indicated on the fireworks.
Utilize an incense stick to ignite the fireworks.
It is imperative to consistently wear eye protection when igniting fireworks.
Keep a container of water or a garden hose readily available in case of fire or any other incident.

Don'ts

Avoid attempting to re-ignite or handle fireworks that have not fully ignited.
Avoid shooting fireworks in metal or glass containers.
Avoid aiming or hurling fireworks at another individual.
Refrain from wearing loose clothing while handling fireworks.
Avoid carrying fireworks in your pockets.
Following a fireworks display, refrain from picking up any remaining fireworks, as they may still be active.
